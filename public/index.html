What is IAF？

IAF(ICP Asia Fund) is an ecosystem fund of up to $15M, managed by SNZ Capital with the Dfinity Foundation in partnership.

The fund will invest in and support early-stage projects in DeFi, NFT, and infrastructure that developing on the Internet Computer, with a particular focus on projects from Asia.

What can IAF provide for applicants?

-Provide financial support
-Provide technical support
-Provide community part-time and full-time positions

What project is more likely to apply for IAF funding？

-High code efficiency, large-scale application-oriented projects.
-Community-centric, web3-based project.
-Asian Dfinity ecological projects are prioritized.
